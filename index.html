Simple Omlette Recipe

An omelet is a dish you might order for breakfast or brunch â€” it's kind of like a folded pancake made of beaten eggs, sometimes with the addition of vegetables, cheese, or meat. Yum. A good omelet is a little bit fluffy, but firm enough to contain its filling.

Preparation Time

  • Total Approximetely : 10min
  • Preparation : 5 minutes
  • Cooking : 4 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs, milk, salt and pepper in medium bowl until blended.
  2. Heat butter in 6 to 8-inch nonstick omelet pan or skillet over medium-high heat until hot. Tilt pan to coat bottom. Pour in egg mixture. Mixture should set immediately at edges.
  3. Gently push cooked portions from edges toward the center with inverted turner so that uncooked eggs can reach the hot pan surface. Continue cooking, tilting pan and gently moving cooked portions as needed.
  4. When top surface of eggs is thickened and no visible liquid egg remains, place filling on one side of the omelet. Fold omelet in half with turner. With a quick flip of the pan
